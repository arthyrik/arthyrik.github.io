About The Conference

The Marketing Code is Silicon Valley’s first conference devoted entirely to technology & B2B marketers.
Marketing has changed drastically over the last five to ten years. We call it The Marketing Code because marketers must approach their marketing efforts in the same way that product teams do with roadmaps or engineering teams with building features — using a mix of creativity, data-driven analysis, testing, and key performance tracking.
